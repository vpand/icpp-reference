   Construct an iterator.
   1) Value-initializes the underlying iterator current_ via its default
   member initializer (= ranges::iterator_t<Base>()).
   2) Initializes the underlying iterator current_ with
   std::move(current).

   Initializes the underlying iterator current_ with std::move(i.current).
