   The Mathematical Special Functions library was originally part of
   Library TR1 ISO/IEC TR 19768:2007, then published as an independent ISO
   standard, ISO/IEC 29124:2010, and finally merged to ISO C++ as of
   C++17.

   See Mathematical special functions for the ISO/IEC 29124:2010 version
   of this library.

Notes

   The float and long double overloads for math special functions without
   the "f" or "l" suffix are present in the final draft of ISO/IEC
   29124:2010 (N3060), but absent in the published C++17/20 standard (see
   LWG issue 3234). These overloads were not provided by MSVC STL until VS
   2022 17.3.

   These functions are unrelated to special member functions of class
   types.
